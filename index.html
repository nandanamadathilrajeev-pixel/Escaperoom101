The Experience

What's An Escape Room?

An escape room is a live, immersive puzzle experience where you and your team are "locked" inside a themed room. Using only your wits, communication, and teamwork, you must uncover hidden clues, decode cryptic messages, and solve interconnected puzzles — all before the clock hits zero.

01

Book Online

Choose your game from our lineup of immersive experiences and reserve your time slot. Groups of 2–8 welcome.

02

Get Locked In

You and your crew are sealed inside the room. The clock starts — 60 or 75 minutes to uncover the mystery.

03

Find Clues & Solve Puzzles

Work together to discover hidden clues, crack combination locks, and solve interconnected puzzles.
